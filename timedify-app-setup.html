How to add the Timedify App Block

Important: For security reasons, this page cannot automatically open the Theme Editor in your Shopify admin.
Tip: For the best experience, open your Shopify Admin in a new browser tab.
This way, you can follow the instructions here while setting up the Timedify App Block in your store.

Watch this video to learn how to add the Timedify App Block to your Shopify store:

Follow these steps to add the Timedify App Block using the Theme Editor:

  1. Access the Theme Editor: Go to your Shopify Admin, navigate to Online Store > Themes, and click Customize on your active theme.
  2. Select a Section: Choose the page or section (e.g., Homepage, Product Page) where you want to add the block.
  3. Add the Block: Click + Add block, find the Timedify App Block, and add it to the section.
  4. Configure Settings: Adjust the block’s settings (schedule, content, etc.) as needed.
  5. Save Changes: Click Save to apply the block to your store.

How to Use the Timedify App Block

Watch this video to learn how to configure the Timedify App Block:

Once the App Block is added, configure it to display dynamic, scheduled content with these steps:

  1. Set the Schedule: Control when your content is visible by setting specific dates or times.
  2. Add Content: Include text, images, videos, or custom HTML to showcase in the block.
  3. Choose Element Order: Arrange the content elements in your preferred sequence.
  4. Enable Countdown: Activate a countdown timer for time-sensitive promotions or events.
  5. Customize Colors and Alignment: Adjust the block’s appearance to match your store’s branding.

Preview your changes in the Theme Editor and test the block on your store’s frontend to ensure everything works as expected.
